Inside Look At Our 12 Week Course

Education is freedom. Before joining, client's struggle to successfully navigate their fitness journey on their own because they don't know what to do or how to do it for themselves. We put a stop to the cycle by educating our girls on all things what, how, and WHY.

Week 1: Foundation & Goal Setting

Establish your baseline, set realistic goals, and learn the fundamentals of sustainable fitness.

Week 2: Nutrition Basics

Understanding macros, micronutrients, and how to build balanced meals without restriction.

Week 3: Training Principles

Learn proper form, progressive overload, and how to structure your workouts for maximum results.

Week 4: Mindset & Habits

Breaking old patterns, building consistency, and developing a healthy relationship with food and fitness.

Week 5: Advanced Nutrition

Meal timing, pre/post workout nutrition, and how to navigate social situations while staying on track.

Week 6: Training Optimization

Periodization, deload weeks, and how to adjust your training based on your body's feedback.

Week 7: Recovery & Sleep

The importance of rest, stress management, and how sleep impacts your progress.

Week 8: Troubleshooting Plateaus

What to do when progress stalls, how to make adjustments, and staying motivated long-term.

Week 9: Lifestyle Integration

Traveling, dining out, holidays, and maintaining your results in real-life situations.

Week 10: Metabolic Health

Understanding your metabolism, reverse dieting, and sustainable maintenance strategies.

Week 11: Long-Term Planning

Creating your own protocols, independent decision making, and setting future goals.

Week 12: Graduation & Beyond

Celebrating your progress, maintenance strategies, and continuing your journey with confidence.

Meet Our Head Coach

Hey guys! My name is Alejandra

I am the head coach and owner behind Elevate Her. My WHY behind this program stems from more than just passion, it comes from lived experience.

Looking back at where I started, I wasted years of my time, energy, and confidence chasing quick fixes - all for what I thought was the only way to achieve the body of my dreams.

Yet because my methods were never sustainable to begin with, any progress I made as a result was equally as temporary. And although I restricted, sacrificed, and said no more times than I could count for YEARS, I had little to no progress to show for it.

What I've come to realize now is that I wasn't lacking more discipline or willpower... I was simply following strategies designed for short term results, not long term success.

In 2021 I finally made a decision to put an end to that cycle. To stop chasing "skinny at all costs" and instead build a body and lifestyle I could maintain with freedom and confidence.

Fast forward to 2025, I'm beyond grateful to have not only transformed my own approach but to now hold the skillset, experience, and knowledge to help other women avoid making the same mistakes I did.

Because Elevate Her isn't about throwing another diet your way. It's about proving that you don't have to cut out the foods you love, spend endless hours in the gym, or sacrifice your sanity just to see results. What you actually need is structure, education, and accountability that teaches you how real life and real results can coexist without compromise.
