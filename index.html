Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the program cost?

Investment details are shared during our application call. I customize packages based on your specific needs and goals, which is why I don't list a one-size-fits-all price. What I can tell you: this is a premium coaching experience for women who are ready to make a real investment in themselves. If you're tired of wasting money on programs that don't work, this is different. Apply here and we'll discuss details on our call.

How is this different from other fitness programs?

Most programs give you a PDF and wish you luck. Elevate Her is hands-on, personalized coaching. You get weekly check-ins, real-time adjustments, 24/7 access to me, and a complete education in how your body works. Plus, we focus on sustainability—no extreme restriction, no hours of cardio, just results that last.

Do I need a gym membership?

Nope! I create custom programs for both gym and home setups. Whether you have a full gym or just a pair of dumbbells, we make it work.

What if I travel frequently or have a crazy schedule?

That's exactly why this program works. Your plan is built around YOUR life, not the other way around. You'll learn how to stay on track during travel, busy seasons, and life's chaos. I've coached clients through weddings, vacations, and work trips—they still get results.

Will I have to give up my favorite foods?

Absolutely not. This isn't a diet—it's a lifestyle. You'll learn how to include the foods you love while still making progress. Pizza, pasta, wine? All possible. The goal is flexibility, not restriction.

How soon will I see results?

Most clients notice changes in energy, strength, and how their clothes fit within the first 2-3 weeks. Visible physical changes typically appear around weeks 4-6. By week 12, you'll be a completely different person—inside and out.

What happens after the 12 weeks?

You keep your course access for life, and many clients continue with ongoing coaching at a reduced rate. But the goal is to teach you everything you need to maintain your results independently. No dependency, just empowerment.
